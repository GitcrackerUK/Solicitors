about us

We are a small progressive firm of Solicitors committed to providing our clients with a prompt, professional and friendly service of the very highest quality.

We pride ourselves on being easily contactable and approachable, whilst providing confidential legal advice and services at competitive prices.

Most new clients come to us on the strength of personal recommendations, which is a testament to the quality of the service we provide.

In May 2011 the firm was accredited by the Law Society with membership of the Conveyancing Quality Scheme. This is a quality standard which shows that we commit to good practice management and we adhere to prudent and efficient conveyancing procedures.

Our Accreditations

The clinical negligence accreditation covers all work undertaken, arising out of any act of negligence.

The Law Society's Children Law Accreditation covers all types of children law work.

The Criminal Litigation Accreditation covers all types of criminal law work.

The Family Law Advanced Accreditation covers specialist areas of family law work.

The Immigration Law Advanced accreditation covers all types of immigration law work.

The Family Law Accreditation covers all types of family law work, except public law Children Act work
