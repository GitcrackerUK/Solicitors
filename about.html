About goodwood solicitors

Goodwood was established on 1st November 2000 and celebrates 20 years trading on 1st November 2020. The firm’s strengths has been to support large and small businesses from purchasing companies to dealing with business start ups, raising finance for their businesses and proving bespoke Legal Services in the Royal Town of Sutton Coldfield. We are on the panel of all the leading Banks and Building Societies.

Our legal advice and support combines technical excellence with a sound commercial perspective, ensuring that we are able to meet the expectations of our clients for all their legal services.

Goodwood moved into their flagship offices following their steady expansion drive in a listed office building which was the former residence of the last Town Mayor of Sutton Coldfield in 1950.

Understanding our clients and their business is the cornerstone of our success and this approach ensures we can provide specialist legal support and advice based on the best strategy.
